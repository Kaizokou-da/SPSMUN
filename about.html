Welcome to SPSMUN

Get ready for an extraordinary experience as we unveil the 5th edition of SPSMUN, where bigger and better is not just a motto, but a way of life. Brace yourself for an immersive journey that will challenge your limits and ignite your passion for diplomacy.

Each year, we push the boundaries of traditional conferences by diversifying our committees, ensuring fresh perspectives, and introducing exciting challenges. Prepare to be captivated as you navigate through dynamic simulations, engaging debates, and overall, a groundbreaking conference.

Our mission is to equip you with life skills that go beyond the classroom. Through our carefully crafted programs, we aim to instil in you the power of effective communication, the art of critical thinking, and the importance of being an active global citizen.

SPSMUN is where students from various schools converge, uniting their passion and determination to make the world a better place. With SPSMUN, you'll forge connections that transcend borders, cultures, and backgrounds, building a network of like-minded individuals who share your vision for positive change.
