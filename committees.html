Committees at SPSMUN

Explore pressing agendas from global governance to cultural identity.

WTO

Reviewing the Global Trade Impact of United States Tariff Measures with Special Emphasis on Multilateral Economic Stability

The WTO will analyze how recent US tariff policies affect global trade flows, supply chains, and the stability of the multilateral economic order. Delegates will debate the ripple effects on developing and developed economies alike.

CSW

Addressing Online Gender-Based Discrimination and Safeguarding the Digital Rights of Women and Girls

The CSW will confront the growing challenge of online gender-based discrimination, exploring policy solutions to protect the digital rights, privacy, and safety of women and girls in the digital age.

Explore CSW →

UNHRC

Deliberation on the Human Rights Implications of Artificial Intelligence in State Surveillance and Law Enforcement Practices

UNHRC will debate the ethical and human rights concerns arising from the use of AI in state surveillance and law enforcement, focusing on privacy, discrimination, and accountability in the age of intelligent technologies.

Explore UNHRC →

Cabinet Crisis Committee

POK (Pakistan Occupied Kashmir) Declares Independence with China's Backing

In a high-stakes scenario, POK declares independence with support from China. Delegates must navigate diplomatic, military, and humanitarian challenges as the crisis rapidly unfolds.

UNSC

Reviewing the Legal and Ethical Dimensions of Targeted Assassinations as a Counter-Terrorism Strategy

The UNSC will examine the legality, morality, and effectiveness of targeted assassinations in counter-terrorism, weighing state security against international law and human rights.

Explore UNSC →

Lok Sabha

Debating the National Implementation of the Uniform Civil Code

Lok Sabha will engage in a critical debate on the prospects, challenges, and societal impact of implementing a Uniform Civil Code across India, balancing constitutional ideals with cultural diversity.
